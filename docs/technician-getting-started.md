# FieldTek for Technicians — Getting Started

A plain-English guide to using FieldTek on your phone in the field. No technical background needed.

If something isn't working, your office/admin has a
[troubleshooting guide](pilot-troubleshooting.md) — tell them what you saw on screen.

---

## 1. Log in

1. Your office will send you an **invite email**. Tap the link and set your password.
2. After that, open FieldTek and sign in with your **email and password**.
3. The first time, do this **with a good internet connection** (Wi-Fi or strong signal). FieldTek
   needs one good online start to set itself up for offline use later.

You'll land on **My Jobs** — your list of assigned work.

---

## 2. Install FieldTek on your phone (recommended)

Installing it makes FieldTek open like a normal app (full screen, its own icon) and is what makes
**offline mode** work reliably.

### iPhone (use Safari)
1. Open the FieldTek web address in **Safari** (not Chrome — on iPhone only Safari can install it).
2. Log in and let **My Jobs** finish loading.
3. Tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
4. Open FieldTek from the **new home-screen icon once while online**. This first open sets up offline mode.

### Android (use Chrome)
1. Open the FieldTek web address in **Chrome**.
2. Log in and let **My Jobs** finish loading.
3. Tap **Install app** when it appears (or the **⋮** menu → **Install app / Add to Home screen**).
4. Open FieldTek from the **new home-screen icon once while online**.

> **Important:** the very first launch must be **online**. After that, FieldTek can open and work
> even with no signal.

---

## 3. Find and open your jobs

- Your jobs are under **My Jobs** (the main screen after login).
- Jobs are grouped into **Today**, **Upcoming**, and **Completed**.
- **Tap a job** to open it. You'll see the customer, address, what to do, and — if your office set
  one up — a **checklist** of steps.

## 4. Complete checklist items

- Each step has a **big checkbox**. **Tap it** to mark the step done. It turns green and gets a line
  through it.
- Tap a step's **title** to expand it and add details.

## 5. Save notes on a step

1. Expand the step (tap its title).
2. Type in the **"Add notes for this step…"** box.
3. Tap **Save Notes**.
4. You'll get a quick confirmation, and the note shows under the step.

## 6. Update a job's status

Use the status control on the job (e.g. **Start Job → In Progress → Complete**). FieldTek confirms
the change. Completing a job may ask you for completion notes.

---

## 7. Online, offline, and syncing — what the badges mean

FieldTek shows a small badge (in the header, and a fuller panel on My Jobs) so you always know
whether your work is saved to the office yet:

| What you see | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **No badge** (or "All synced") | You're online and everything is saved to the office. | Nothing — you're good. |
| **Offline** | No internet right now. You can keep working; changes are saved on your phone. | Keep working. They'll send up when you reconnect. |
| **Syncing…** | FieldTek is sending your saved changes to the office. | Wait a moment — keep the app open. |
| **_N_ pending** | _N_ changes are saved on your phone, waiting to go to the office. | Get back into coverage and open the app; watch it drain to 0. |
| **Sync error** | One or more changes had trouble sending. | Open My Jobs to see which. Often it clears itself on the next try; if not, see below. |

**When you make a change offline**, you'll see a message like **"Saved offline. Will sync when
connected."** That means it's safely on your phone and will upload automatically once you have signal
(with the app open).

> On iPhone especially, changes only upload while the **app is open**. Habit to build:
> **back in coverage → open FieldTek → watch the pending count go to 0.**

---

## 8. Before signing out on a shared phone/tablet

If you share a device with other technicians:

1. **First, make sure everything is synced** — pending count at **0**, no "Sync error".
   Signing out **discards** anything still waiting to sync.
2. Then tap **Sign Out** (bottom of the menu).

Signing out wipes your jobs and saved data off that device so the next person can't see them. That's
why you sync **first**.

---

## 9. If FieldTek says "New version available"

You'll occasionally see a **"New version available"** message with a **Reload** button.

- If you have **changes waiting to sync**, FieldTek will warn you ("Unsynced changes pending") and
  let you choose **Reload anyway** or **Not yet**. Prefer to **sync first** (get the pending count to
  0), then reload.
- If you're **offline**, it'll tell you to reconnect first.
- Otherwise, tap **Reload** to get the latest version. It only takes a second.

## 10. If something doesn't sync

1. Check the badge. **Offline** or **Syncing…** just means "not yet" — get back into coverage and
   keep the app open.
2. If you see **Sync error** or a count that won't go down:
   - Make sure you actually have internet (open any website).
   - Keep the FieldTek app **open and in the foreground** for a minute.
   - Try the **Sync** button on the My Jobs status panel if shown.
3. If FieldTek shows a red message like **"Offline change could not sync"** that names a job and says
   a change "was removed" — that change couldn't be sent after many tries. **Tell your office/support**
   and mention which job it named, so they can check whether anything's missing. (You can tap
   **Dismiss** to clear the message.)
4. Still stuck? Tell support: your **name/email**, the **job name**, what the **badge** said, and
   roughly **when** it happened. The [troubleshooting guide](pilot-troubleshooting.md) lists exactly
   what helps them.

---

You don't need to understand the technical side — if a screen looks wrong or stuck, take a quick
screenshot and send it to your office with a one-line note. That's always enough to get help.
